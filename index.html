About

Covid Slug is a faculty and student tracking system for COVID-19. This website includes links to websites
such as local testing sites (not just on campus), coronavirus symptoms, guidelines for what to do when
testing positive, updated quarantine information, local hospital contact information for serious cases, and
when to seek medical care. The information is displayed in a visually appealing manner to ensure that such
information is easily understood. This system makes crucial health and safety information more accessible
to the UCSC community.
